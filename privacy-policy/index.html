Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This policy covers how we handle your information when you visit our website and use our free resources. If you become a client, data handling for service delivery is covered by a separate data handling agreement.

1. Who We Are

DayOnePros is an operations consultancy operated by Carol Leung, based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We help small business owners build systems that take the admin off their plate, from automated follow-ups to workflow design and process improvement.

For privacy questions, contact us at carol@dayonepros.com.

2. What Information We Collect

Information When We Collect It Why
Name and email address When you download a free resource or fill out a form To deliver the resource and send follow-up communications you opted into
Name, email, and meeting details When you book a scope call To schedule and prepare for the call
Website usage data When you browse our website To understand how people use our site and improve it

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, government IDs, or health information through our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver free resources you've requested (calculators, guides, etc.)
  • Send email communications you've opted into (tips, resources, and occasional updates about our services)
  • Schedule and conduct scope calls
  • Improve our website and understand what content is helpful

We will never use your information to send unsolicited communications. All email communications require your explicit opt-in consent.

4. CASL Compliance

We comply with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). This means:

  • We only send commercial emails to people who have given explicit consent
  • Every email includes a clear unsubscribe option
  • We honour unsubscribe requests promptly (within 10 business days as required by law, though we aim for immediately)
  • All emails identify DayOnePros as the sender and include our contact information

5. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Period.

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and deliver our resources:

Service Purpose What They Access
Google Forms Collecting form submissions Name, email, and responses you submit
Google Workspace (Gmail, Sheets) Email delivery and contact management Name and email address
Google Calendar Scope call scheduling Name, email, and meeting details
GitHub Pages Website hosting Standard web server logs
YouTube (embedded videos) Video content Standard YouTube tracking (governed by Google's privacy policy)

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Third-party embeds (such as YouTube videos) may set their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored in Google Workspace services (Gmail, Google Sheets, Google Drive), which are hosted on Google's servers. Google maintains industry-standard security certifications. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as you remain subscribed to our communications or until you request deletion. If you unsubscribe, we will retain only the minimum information needed to honour your unsubscribe preference (your email address on our suppression list).

You can request deletion of your information at any time by emailing carol@dayonepros.com.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from email communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email carol@dayonepros.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are designed for business owners and are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
