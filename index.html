About Me

I've been solving tech problems on my own since I was a kid. I was self-teaching HTML and CSS, building websites and fansites from scratch, troubleshooting my own virus issues, rolling back system versions, and figuring things out long before there was a tutorial for every problem.

Life took me down a different path before I ended up here. Over the past decade, I worked across government, healthcare, and compliance — coordinating complex operations, managing financial systems, and building the kind of process-heavy workflows that keep organizations running. I learned firsthand how much time, energy, and money gets lost when systems aren't built right.

Along the way, I worked alongside small service businesses — the kind that are too busy hustling to optimize how they operate. I saw firsthand how missed leads, scheduling chaos, and endless admin quietly eat into revenue and burn out the people running the show.

That's why I founded DayOnePros. I combine my technical background with my operational experience to build AI-powered systems that do the repetitive, revenue-killing work for you — so you can focus on what actually grows your business.

If you're a service business owner sitting on untapped leads, outdated customer lists, or a workflow that's stuck in the past — let's talk.

— Carol, Founder
