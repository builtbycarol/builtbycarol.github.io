How I work

First, I listen.

I start by understanding how your business actually runs. Not the big-picture strategy stuff. The ground-level reality. Where are you spending time on things that could happen automatically? Where are things slipping through the cracks? I find those gaps.

Then, I build.

I design and build the systems that close those gaps. Automated follow-ups so leads don't go cold. Intake forms that route requests to the right person. Reminders that go out without you having to remember. Nothing flashy. Just things that work, every time, without you thinking about it.

And I stick around.

I'm not a one-and-done contractor. I work alongside you on an ongoing basis, finding new bottlenecks as your business grows and building the systems to handle them. Think of me as your operations department, without the overhead of a full-time hire.

What it looks like in practice

Mike — Home Inspector

From zero online presence to fully operational in under two weeks.

Mike was starting a home inspection business from scratch. No website, no email, no Google presence, no way for customers to find or book him. I built his full digital foundation: professional website, business email, Google Business Profile, and an online booking system. He went from invisible to findable in less than two weeks. People now tell him his business looks established and professional.

Taylor — Insurance Broker

A charity event that actually captured leads instead of losing them.

Taylor was sponsoring a charity hockey tournament. Great networking opportunity, but no system for collecting contacts or following up afterward. I built an intake form for the event, automated email sequences for post-event follow-up, and print materials to tie it all together. Instead of collecting business cards and losing half of them, every contact went into a system that followed up automatically.
